What the Audit Shows (Context for You)

You don't need to explain all of this on the first call. This is background so you can answer questions intelligently.
  1. AI Citation Score — How many times the business shows up in ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, and Gemini responses for relevant local searches.
  2. Listing Accuracy — There are 67+ sites AI uses to confirm a business is legitimate (name, address, phone). Most local businesses have errors, inconsistencies, or are missing entirely from many of them.
  3. Review Signals — How many reviews, which platforms, and whether reviews are being responded to (AI rewards response activity).
  4. Social Media Visibility — Whether AI can read and index their social profiles. A wrong privacy setting or missing business page = invisible to AI.
  5. Website Load Speed — AI crawlers skip pages that take more than 2 seconds to load on mobile. Most local business sites fail this.
  6. Schema / Structured Data — Whether the website has the technical code that tells AI what the business does and where it is located.

Objection Handling

"I already have someone doing my SEO / marketing."
"That's great, and this is actually designed to work alongside them, not replace them. What most SEO agencies focus on is your website and traditional search rankings. What we focus on is specifically how AI platforms like ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, and Gemini are reading and recommending your business. Those are two different things, and a lot of businesses are putting all their money into traditional SEO and getting skipped completely by AI search because of listing errors and missing signals outside the website. We're filling that gap."
"I can't afford another monthly expense right now."
"I totally understand, and I don't want to add something that doesn't pay for itself. That's why we start with the free audit — we want you to actually see the gaps first and decide if it makes sense. A lot of members are surprised how much business they're losing right now because AI isn't recommending them. At $397 a month, one or two new customers a month from AI search covers it. But again, let's look at your audit together first and you can make that call with real information."
"I'm not sure how much AI search really matters for my type of business."
"That's a fair question. Here's what we're seeing: Google AI now has over 2 billion monthly users. ChatGPT is close to that. When someone types 'best [your service] near me' into any of these platforms, it gives them a list of businesses. Right now you cannot pay to be at the top of that list — it's completely based on how well AI can read and trust your business data. The businesses that get recommended are the ones that have their digital foundation clean and consistent. The audit will actually show you right now where you stand, so it's not a guess."
"How is this different from what Google already does?"
"Google AI Overviews are actually one of the platforms we optimize for — but it's not just Google anymore. ChatGPT, Gemini, Apple's AI, Perplexity, Microsoft Copilot — people are using all of these to find local businesses now. Each one pulls from different data sources. Our job is to make sure your business data is accurate, consistent, and readable across all of them. Google Search Console helps with your website, but it doesn't fix your listings on those 67 other sites that AI uses to verify you."
"I need to think about it / talk to my partner."
"Of course, absolutely. Can I ask — is there any specific concern that's making you hesitate, or is it just a matter of timing? I want to make sure you have everything you need to make a good decision. What I'd suggest is we send you over the audit report so you have it in writing, and you can review it together. Would that help? And is there a good day later this week when it might make sense to reconnect?"
"How long before I see results?"
"Most clients start seeing movement within 30 days — you'll see your listing scores improving and your AI citation numbers going up. Full results typically build over 90 days, which is actually why we structured the short-term option around 3 months. Some AI platforms like ChatGPT refresh their index every 90 days, so that's the natural window. You'll see it in your weekly reports, and we'll walk through it with you on your monthly call."
"Can I just do this myself?"
"You can, and we'll actually show you exactly what needs to be fixed in the audit. The challenge is the citation cleanup alone takes about 300 hours to do manually — and every time you change an address, phone number, or anything else, you have to do it all over again. We manage that on an ongoing basis so you don't have to. But yes, the audit is yours to keep either way."
